The Cause Of Childhood Obesity

-------------------------------------------------------------------

The main cause of childhood overweight and obesity is basically due to people eating too much, and then not excersising to use up the energy.

This is happening more and more these days, as it is so much easier to not excersise, and to stay inside all day. Also due to the easiness of accessing unhealthy high calory food means that people will become more obese more easily.
