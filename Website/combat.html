How To Combat Obesity

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I have come up with just a few ways that you can do to combat obesity

-------------------------------

  • Buy a gym membership
  • Buy vegetables, not fast food
  • Try and buy local vegetables
  • Get healthy snacks
  • Do some (more) exercise

    • All of the above will also help lower the risk of heart attacks and strokes when you are older

    --------------------------------------------------------------------

    I have come up with a few things that the government should do to combat obesity too.

    You can send your names here to sign the petition

    -------------------------------

  • Increase fast food tax
  • Reduce vegetable prices
  • Give funds to farmers to grow vegetables
  • Adverts on TV for fast food should be banned
  • Much more exercise in school
  • Healthier canteens at schools
  • Cheaper gym memberships
  • No sugary food advertised at checkouts
  • Get popular people to represent healthy food companies
